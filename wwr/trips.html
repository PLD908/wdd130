Our Rafting Adventures

Choose your perfect rafting experience

Family Adventure

Perfect for beginners & families

Experience the joy of rafting with our beginner-friendly trip. Safe, exciting, and perfect for families with children aged 8 and up. Enjoy gentle rapids and beautiful scenery along a 3-mile stretch of river.

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Min Age: 8 years
  • Group Size: 4-12 people

Half-Day Adventure

Our most popular trip

Perfect for thrill-seekers looking for an exciting half-day adventure. Navigate through class II and III rapids while enjoying spectacular canyon views and wildlife sightings.

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Min Age: 12 years
  • Group Size: 6-14 people

Expert Rapids Challenge

For experienced rafters only

Take on our most challenging rapids in this full-day adventure. Navigate class IV and V rapids through stunning gorges and remote wilderness areas. Previous rafting experience required.

  • Duration: 8 hours
  • Difficulty: Expert
  • Min Age: 16 years
  • Group Size: 6-8 people
